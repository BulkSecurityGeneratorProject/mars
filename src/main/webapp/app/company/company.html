About Us

Mars Security Ltd. was established to be a niche communications Service Provider focused on providing high level private WAN services for the mass and Mid-Sized organisations.

The company was established by a passionate group of individuals with extensive knowledge and experience in both voice and data communications. The directors are intimately involved with the development and ongoing success of the business and have an engineering background with specific experience in the design and management of communications networks for businesses.

Mars Security has grown to service a significant number of national and foreign clients and currently connects many different types of businesses into the network. We measure our success by the fact that each and every one of our clients is prepared to be a reference client to assist us in securing additional business, as well as our record of 100% uptime of our core switch network.

Mars concentrates on its market niche to provide individuals and professional business grade data services to mid-sized businesses. We provide very high service levels and exceptional value for money for this market. Our credentials are well proven by our existing client base and matched by our extensive expertise in this area.

Mars Security is passionate about ensuring our clients get the best technical and commercial deal from the resources of the major carriers by means of our wholesale relationships with all of them.
